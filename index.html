GRAMMAR Simple Past — The Time Machine
pp. 79–80  |  10 min
TYPE FORM AFFIRMATIVE ✓ NEGATIVE ✗ QUESTION ?
Regular verb + -ed She visited Paris. She didn't visit Paris. Did she visit Paris?
Regular verb + -ed They stayed at a hotel. They didn't stay there. Did they stay there?
Irregular changes form He went to the beach. He didn't go there. Did he go there?
Irregular changes form We ate amazing food. We didn't eat there. Did you eat there?
WH Question WH + did + base Where did you go?    What did you do?    Who did you meet?
TIME EXPRESSIONS
yesterday last week last year ago in 2019 last summer
KEY RULES
Regular verbs: add -ed (visit → visited)
Irregular verbs: change completely (go → went)
Negative: didn't + base form
Question: Did + subject + base form
No -ed in negatives or questions!
